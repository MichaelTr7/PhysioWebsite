About Us

North York Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Clinic is a physiotherapist-owned clinic located in North York. We believe the best physiotherapy experience requires expert physiotherapists providing one-on-one care with evidence-based manual therapy techniques and therapeutic exercises. As leaders in pain relief and injury recovery, our specialized physiotherapists go beyond standard physiotherapy. Our sports injury program treats injured amateur and professional athletes in basketball, soccer, baseball, track, gymnastics, and other disciplines. We also offer massage therapy, acupuncture, chiropractic care, vestibular rehabilitation, custom-made orthotics, custom-made braces, orthopedic shoes, and shockwave therapy.
